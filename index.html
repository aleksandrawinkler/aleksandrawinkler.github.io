Research

Galactic Archaeology

High-Resolution Stellar Spectra of Wide Binary Systems

Currently, I am conducting research under the mentorship of Dr. Catherine Manea (NSF Fellow). Our work focuses on the study of wide binary star systems.

By analyzing high-resolution stellar spectra for each star in these pairs, we aim to uncover chemical signatures that provide insights into the history of the Milky Way—a field often referred to as Galactic Archaeology.
